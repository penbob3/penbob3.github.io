My Car! 2003 Daihatsu Copen
JB-DET | 660cc 4-Cylinder DOHC | 67hp - 100Nm
A wonderful Australian-delivered example of the world's
first convertible Kei car with an electric hardtop.
It's equipped with a 5-speed manual and has been modified
with a GruppeM Power Cleaner intake.
The custom Japan-style KEIPUP number plate is expensive
but it just doesn't look right without it!
